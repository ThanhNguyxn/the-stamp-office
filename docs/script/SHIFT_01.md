# SHIFT 01 — Orientation

**Target Duration:** 8–10 minutes  
**Tone:** Welcoming. Slightly off.

---

## Core Mechanic Introduced
**Basic Stamping** — APPROVED / DENIED only. Read ticket, check rule, stamp.

---

## Horror Beat
The lights flicker once when you stamp your 5th ticket. No acknowledgment. Resume normal.

---

## Comedy Beat
System toast appears after first DENIED stamp:  
> "Request denied. Somewhere, a form weeps."

---

## Special Customer

**The Orientation Guide**  
A floating clipboard with eyes. Speaks in bullet points. Claims to be "temporary." Has been here for 47 years. Leaves after teaching you to stamp without saying goodbye.

---
